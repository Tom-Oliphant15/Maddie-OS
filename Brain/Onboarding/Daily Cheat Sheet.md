# Maddie OS, Daily Cheat Sheet

*What to say, when. Living version of the printed cheat sheet in `Assets/`. Read it anytime by saying "cheat sheet", "what do I say for X", or "/help".*

---

## Start the day

Say **morning**, **hey**, **hi**. Fires your Daily Briefing.

Working on one brand all morning? Say **let's work on Stride**, **switch to Fade Golf**.

---

## Drafting content

| You want to... | Just say... |
|---|---|
| Plan a whole campaign | `expand campaign for [topic]` |
| Brief a shoot | `filming brief for Luke swing reels` |
| Brief an ambassador to post | `ambassador brief for Luke` |
| Build a landing page | `landing page brief for [topic]` |
| Turn one post into ten | `repurpose this for Facebook and TikTok` |
| Draft an email or Slack | `draft an email to Luke about Friday` |
| Capture an idea fast | `add to notes: [thought]` |

---

## Check before you publish

| You want to... | Just say... |
|---|---|
| Everything at once | `is this ready to ship` |
| Just the words | `proofread this: [text]` |
| Just the tone | `does this match the Stride voice` |
| Just the design | `visual check this` |
| Full agent-chain improvement | `critique this [filepath]` |

---

## Thinking partner

- Pressure-test a plan before it ships: `grill me on this`
- Prep a hard conversation: `help me prep for a difficult conversation with [name]`
- Park work mid-task: `save this for later`

---

## Paid ads

- Headlines: `google ads variations for [topic]`
- Keywords: `keywords for stride nail surgery`

---

## Reporting and data

- Weekly Slack post: `weekly report`
- Month end: `monthly report for May`
- Raw numbers: `pull the data for Stride this week`

---

## Meetings

- Prep: `meeting prep for Tom Zoom tomorrow`
- Process recordings: `check my meetings`
- Capture an idea: `add to notes: [thought]`

---

## Look something up

- Research a tactic, tool, or topic: `research [topic]` or `is [thing] any good`
- Find something in Slack: `search Slack for [thing]`

---

## End the day

Say **done**, **that's it**, **wrap up**. Fires Session End and saves everything.

---

## When something's off

Describe the problem in plain English. The OS will usually route itself.

Or say `/diagnostics`.

Still stuck? Ping Tom in Slack `#leadership`. Tell him what you tried.

---

## Brain and persona work (less frequent)

- New audience persona: `audience persona for [brand + topic]`
- OS feels stale: `/learning`
- Trends scan: `social trends`

---

**Don't see what you need? Say `/help`. The OS will find the right skill or walk you through it.**
