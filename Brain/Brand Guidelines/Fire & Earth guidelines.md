## Colour palette

**Primary:** `#b01227` (F&E red)
**Secondary:** `#181206` (near-black), `#ffffff` (white)
**Accent (sparingly):** `#bfb196` (warm neutral)
**Backgrounds:** light or dark depending on which logo variant is being used
**Avoid:** green (Fade Golf), blue (Stride). Generic spa-pastels that pull the brand toward chain-wellness territory.

## Logo

**Where required:** all content F&E produces, physical or social
**Background rule:** logo always on a light or dark background, depending on which logo variant is used
**Clear space / minimum size:** to be confirmed (see Canva brand kit)

## Imagery style

**Use:** real clinic photos. Friendly team photos. Action shots of different services. Premium feel.
**Avoid:** stock wellness imagery (generic woman-on-yoga-mat, detached candle close-ups). Clunky posts.
**Composition preferences:** premium feel that earns trust — show that the therapists are experienced and can get people out of pain. Events should be written up as proper round-ups with descriptions of what happened on the day, plus videos of therapists, not posted as raw clutter.

## Asset-type specifics

### Instagram feed (1080x1080)

People respond better to content with staff and services in frame than to static posts. Need more content of services and the staff. Grid needs to match and look premium.
