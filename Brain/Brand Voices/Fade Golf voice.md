# Fade Golf — Voice

*STATUS: Live. Populated from Maddie OS Bootstrap Form (Maddie, 21 May 2026). Reviewed in-person 22 May.*

## Tone

Trustworthy, expert, premium, warm and welcoming, fun, aspirational but accessible, confident without being arrogant. Knows what it is. Not stuffy, not trying-too-hard, not laddy.

## Vocabulary

**Use:**
- Pro level
- Dream swing
- Get assessed
- Find out what's holding you back
- Best version of your game
- Our coaches
- Elite
- Part of the Fade family
- Tournament ready

**Avoid:**
- Revolutionary
- Lads
- Smash it
- Banter
- Crushing it
- Clients (use "members" or "golfers")
- Packages (use "sessions" or "coaching")
- Facility (use "Sim" or just "Fade Golf")

## Sentence Rhythm

To be drawn from the reference posts below as they get used. Keep premium pacing, no exclamation pile-ons, no "Hey guys" openers.

## Reference Posts (locked good examples)

- [Client story post](https://www.instagram.com/p/DVbwOwojj7r/) — strong because it follows a real journey. Even stronger when first-swing vs latest-swing clips are included.
- [Pain / injury angle post](https://www.instagram.com/p/DUI4lzaiNN_/) — shows Fade can help people in pain or injured. "Back to peak or better."

## Bad Examples (drift catchers)

- [Pro vs amateur match concept](https://www.instagram.com/p/DUlqqYtjnp5/) — concept can be appealing but people likely will not invest the time to watch. The format does not earn its length.

## Cross-Brand Drift Check

- Compared to Stride: should be less clinical, more cultural
- Compared to F&E: should be more grounded/premium, less sensory/feminine
