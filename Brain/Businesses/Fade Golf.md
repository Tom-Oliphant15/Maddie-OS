## Audience

**Primary:** Men aged 25 to 60. Local to Leamington and Warwick. Professional-class — JLR, business owners, Aston Martin, similar local employer mix. Family men and men without children. They have the time and budget to spend in a sim seriously working on their swing. Some are injured or are looking to extend their prime golf years.

**Secondary:** Younger men, maybe playing partly for ego, or newer to golf and unsure of their technique or what they are missing.

**What makes them book / buy / sign up (in their own words):**
- "How can I get better at this?"
- "I don't want to be the worst amongst my mates. Where can I get a professional opinion?"

**What stops them booking:**
- They do not know they are doing something wrong (no awareness of the gap)
- Expectation that it's too much money
- Pride
- Older generation not informed about the technology

**Where they hang out online:**
- Instagram and Facebook (primary platforms)
- Follow golf accounts and pro golfers

**Where they hang out offline:**
- Higher-end bars and pubs
- Pubs near or around golf courses or driving ranges

**What they type into Google:**
- "Golf range near me"
- "Golf sim near me"
- "Golfing lessons in Warwick"
- "Golfers near me"
- "Golfers in Leamington"
