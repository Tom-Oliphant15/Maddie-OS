# Guide: Fade Group Brand Voice Coordinates (NN/G 4-Axis)

Inferred NN/G four-axis brand voice positions for Fade Golf, Stride Clinics, and Fire & Earth Leamington, plus context-flex notes per brand. Used by [[Tone Check]] in Step 4.6 (four-axis coordinate check). Extracted from the skill file 2026-05-21 to keep the skill lean. Methodology baseline: Nielsen Norman Group "Four Dimensions of Tone of Voice in UX Writing".

---

## The four axes (each scored 0 to 10)

| Axis | Low end (0) | High end (10) |
|---|---|---|
| Humor | Funny | Serious |
| Formality | Casual | Formal |
| Respectfulness | Irreverent | Respectful |
| Enthusiasm | Matter-of-fact | Enthusiastic |

---

## Scoring thresholds for Step 4.6

**Pass on this axis:** draft sits within 2 points of brand position on every axis.

**Drift on this axis:** any axis 2 to 4 points off.

**Fail on this axis:** any axis 5+ points off, OR a clinical-info post scoring above 7 enthusiasm (e.g. "Get instant relief now!" on a nail surgery post).
