## Creative direction

**Visual style:** Stride brand visuals, clinical-warm tone. Before-after must have patient permission, mask any identifying detail. Soft lighting, no clinical-cold blue tones.
**Filming brief:** Run /filming-brief for the before-after reel. Need Wendy or Helen on camera demonstrating reassurance, not the procedure itself.
**Hook angles to test:** (1) "What most people try first that does not work" (2) "Why nail surgery is not what you think" (3) "The 3 signs you should not ignore"
**CTA:** Per platform. IG: link in bio to booking page. FB: direct booking link. Google Ads: phone + book online.

## Risks / things to watch

1. **Audience fatigue:** Stride has not posted on nail surgery in 90 days, so this is fresh. Watch for a drop after the mid-month re-emphasis.
2. **Cross-brand overlap:** None. F&E and Fade Golf are not running clinical content this window.
3. **Voice drift:** Stride voice is clinical-warm. The "scarier than it sounds" hook risks drifting to overly casual. Tone Check before publishing.
4. **Compliance:** Before-after requires patient consent in writing. Wendy can confirm consent process.
