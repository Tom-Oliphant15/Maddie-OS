# Guide: Monthly Report Template

Full output template used by [[Monthly Report Draft]]. Extracted from the skill file 2026-05-21 to keep the skill lean. The skill's Step 6 (Compose the report) writes the report against this structure.

---

## Output template

```markdown
# Marketing Monthly Report, [Month Year]

**Period:** [date range]
**Generated:** YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM by Monthly Report Draft
**Author:** Maddie
**For:** Tom, Harry

---

## Headline

[1 paragraph, 3 to 5 sentences. The most important thing about this month for Tom and Harry to walk away knowing. If month was middling, say so plainly.]

---

## Per-brand recap

### Fade Golf

**5 to 7 metrics (goals to actuals):**

| Metric | This month | Target | vs Target | Trend (vs last month) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| New members | [N] | [M] | [delta] | [up / flat / down] |
| Marketing spend | £[N] | £[M] | [under / on / over] | [trend] |
| CPA (new member) | £[N] | £[target if set] | [vs] | [trend] |
| Member renewal rate | [N]% | [M]% | [delta] | [trend] |
| Posts | [N] | [M] | [delta] | [trend] |

**Channel split (only if meaningfully distinct):**
- Organic social: [N] new members / £[N] spend
- Paid search: [N] new members / £[N] spend
- Direct / referral: [N] new members

**Direct-response activity this month:** [campaigns measured this month, results]
**Brand-building activity this month:** [activity done, payback delayed]
**Highlight:** [the most material specific thing]
**Lowlight:** [the most material specific thing, can be "none" if month was clean]
**Attribution caveats:** [what we cannot prove that the numbers might suggest]

### Stride

[Same 5 to 7 metrics table structure.]

**Retention indicator:** returning patient count for the month, repeat-visit rate
**Channel split:** [if relevant]
**Direct-response activity:** [...]
**Brand-building activity:** [...]
**Tag-fix dependency note:** [if Stride conversion data is still unreliable due to tag fix not landing, flag clearly]
**Highlight / Lowlight:** [...]
**Attribution caveats:** [...]

### Fire & Earth Leamington

[Same structure.]

**Retention indicator:** member renewal rate / churn
**Channel split:** [if relevant]
**Direct-response activity:** [...]
**Brand-building activity:** [...]
**Highlight / Lowlight:** [...]
**Attribution caveats:** [...]

---

## Visual snapshot (optional but encouraged)

[One simple chart or screenshot if it replaces 200+ words. Line chart for trend, bar chart for target-vs-actual across brands, screenshot from Sheets / Looker. Skip if no chart adds material clarity.]

---

## Campaign results

For each campaign that ran or completed in the month:

### [Campaign name], [brand]

**Goal:** [from Campaign Brief Expander]
**Status:** completed / in flight / delayed / cancelled
**Spend:** £[N]
**Numbers vs goal:** [if goal was measurable, state the result vs target. State attribution caveats.]
**Lessons:** [1 to 2 sentences on what we learned, applicable to future campaigns]

---

## What worked

[3 to 5 bullets. Specific. Not generic. Each bullet has a why AND a "what we will do differently because of it" hook.]

- [Specific thing that worked + why + so we will [action] in [timeframe]]
- ...

---

## What did not

[2 to 4 bullets. Honest. Critical persona: do not soften.]

- [Specific thing that did not + why + so we will [stop / change / adjust] in [timeframe]]
- ...

---

## Risks for next month

[What is on the horizon that could go wrong. Active risks worth flagging to Tom + Harry now, not in a panic later.]

- [Risk] [likelihood: low / medium / high] [impact if it lands] [what would mitigate]
- ...

---

## Next month priorities

[3 to 5 priorities, ordered. Each one a real commitment, not a wish.]

1. **[Priority]**, [one line on what done looks like, with a date and a number where applicable]
2. **[Priority]**
3. **[Priority]**

---

## Questions for Tom + Harry (decisions needed)

[Things Maddie wants their input on before next month. Each one phrased as a decision, not a topic.]

1. **[Decision needed]:** [context in 1 to 2 lines]. Options: A) [option], B) [option]. Maddie's recommendation: [choice + why].
2. ...

---

## Appendix: data sources and method notes

- Weekly Reports: [list with dates]
- Data Pulls: [list with dates]
- Linked campaigns: [list with file paths]
- **What we can / cannot measure this month:** [explicit caveats, e.g. Stride conversions still unreliable until tag fix, F&E referral source not tracked, Luke ambassador attribution is correlational not causal]
```
