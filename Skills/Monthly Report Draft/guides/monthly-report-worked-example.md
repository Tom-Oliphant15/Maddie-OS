# Guide: Monthly Report Worked Example

Worked example used by [[Monthly Report Draft]]. Extracted from the skill file 2026-05-21 to keep the skill lean. Demonstrates the full template applied to May 2026 reality across all three Fade Group brands: full metrics tables, channel splits, retention indicators, attribution caveats, brand-building vs direct-response separation, action-hook bullets, likelihood-impact-mitigation risks, decision-framed questions.

**Note on maturity:** this example assumes a month where Data Pull is operational and weekly reports have been running. For the actual first runs (May to ~July 2026) Data Pull is a stub pending Metricool MCP, and weekly reports begin wk of 1 June, so the very first monthly will run in **first-month baseline mode** (see Step 1 of the parent skill) with most metrics sourced from manual upstream lookups (Cliniko for Stride, membership tools for Fade Golf, booking records for F&E). Use this example for the mature-month shape; use Step 1 + Step 3 manual-data fallback for the first runs.

---

## Worked example output (abbreviated)

```markdown
# Marketing Monthly Report, May 2026

## Headline

Steady month operationally with the Phase 2 in-person build with Tom delivering the core OS, six skills now live, plus first prototype runs on Stride nail surgery and the Luke ambassador filming. Performance numbers will jump in June once the Stride Google tag fix lands and the per-service Ads restructure goes live. Master Marketing Document drafted, three-way sign-off in two days. May was deliberately a foundation month, not a numbers month.

## Per-brand recap

### Fade Golf

**5 metrics:**

| Metric | This month | Target | vs Target | Trend |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| New members | 4 | 6 | down 2 | flat MoM |
| Marketing spend | £180 | £200 | under | down £80 MoM |
| CPA (new member) | £45 | £40 | over by £5 | up £8 MoM |
| Member renewal rate | 88% | 85% | up 3 pts | flat |
| Posts | 11 | 12 | down 1 | flat |

**Channel split:** all four new members came via organic social + word-of-mouth. No paid this month (Luke filming month, paid restart in June).
**Direct-response activity this month:** none, deliberately. No paid ads ran.
**Brand-building activity this month:** Luke ambassador shoot completed Wed 21, delivered 12 shots + 8 B-roll items. First reels in editing for late-May launch.
**Highlight:** Luke shoot day execution was tight to brief.
**Lowlight:** new-member count below target. Cannot attribute to anything specific in this window, normal-month variance.
**Attribution caveats:** new-member count for May reflects activity from March / April lead-funnel. Luke shoot's effect lands in June and beyond.

### Stride

**6 metrics:**

| Metric | This month | Target | vs Target | Trend |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| New patients | 38 | 45 | down 7 | down 5 MoM |
| Marketing spend | £620 | £600 | over by £20 | flat |
| CPA (new patient) | £16 | £14 | over by £2 | up £3 MoM |
| Returning patients (month) | 92 | 85 | up 7 | up 4 MoM |
| Repeat-visit rate | 71% | 65% | up 6 pts | up 2 pts |
| Posts | 14 | 20 | down 6 | flat |

**Channel split:** 22 new patients via paid search, 11 via organic, 5 via referral.
**Direct-response activity this month:** Generic Stride paid search ran, awaiting tag fix to verify conversion accuracy. Nail surgery campaign prep complete, launch held until tag fix lands.
**Brand-building activity this month:** First Audience Persona file built and locked for nail surgery. Run Club content cadence continued at 4 posts / wk.
**Tag-fix dependency note:** Conversion data still unreliable, Mark fix expected ~28 May. Per-service Ads restructure on hold until verified. May spend reflects estimated tag-corrected CPA, treat as ±20%.
**Highlight:** retention numbers strong, repeat-visit rate up 6 points vs target.
**Lowlight:** posts below target by 6 due to time spent on the OS build + Master Doc drafting. June targets reset to 18 posts to stay realistic given per-service Ads restructure work.
**Attribution caveats:** new patient counts pre-tag-fix carry ±20% uncertainty. Retention numbers are clean (booking system data, not ad-tracking).

### Fire & Earth Leamington

**5 metrics:**

| Metric | This month | Target | vs Target | Trend |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| New members | 3 | 8 | down 5 | down 4 MoM |
| Day passes sold | 19 | 25 | down 6 | flat |
| Marketing spend | £95 | £150 | under | down |
| Member churn (month) | 4 | <3 | over by 1 | up 2 |
| Posts | 5 | 8 | down 3 | flat |

**Channel split:** new members all organic social + walk-in.
**Direct-response activity this month:** none, no paid ran.
**Brand-building activity this month:** marketing canvas walkthrough completed.
**Highlight:** none material this month, foundation building.
**Lowlight:** acquisition down, churn up. Two-month soft pattern, June content angle refresh planned.
**Attribution caveats:** day-pass-to-member conversion not tracked, working blind on that funnel for now.

## What worked

- **Stride retention numbers held up.** Repeat-visit rate at 71% vs 65% target. So we will lean into retention-led content for June (existing-patient stories, treatment journeys, member-of-the-clinic features) to reinforce.
- **Phase 2 in-person build delivered the OS prototype on schedule (Fri 23).** Six skills now in daily use. So we will use June to measure whether OS-driven output is materially sharper than May-style ad-hoc work, with a clear go-no-go on the OS investment at month-end.
- **Luke ambassador shoot day execution was tight to brief.** 12 shots + 8 B-roll items, post sequence already filmed for May 28 to June 4. So we will use the Luke launch as a controlled test of ambassador-led acquisition for Fade Golf.

## What did not

- **Stride post target missed by 6.** I was too deep on the OS build and the Master Doc. So I am resetting June Stride targets to 18 posts to stay realistic given per-service Ads restructure work, and will ringfence post-creation time Mon AM.
- **F&E acquisition down + churn up, two-month soft pattern.** Cannot pin one cause yet. So we will run a content angle refresh by mid-June and review at month-end. If still soft, recommend a platform or positioning rethink at the July Tom + Harry review.

## Risks for next month

- **Stride Google tag fix:** has been "almost done" for 3 weeks. [high likelihood of further slip, high impact]. Mitigation: hard deadline conversation with Mark this week, manual ad-group rebuild as fallback.
- **F&E soft trend continuing:** [medium likelihood of further down month, medium impact]. Mitigation: content angle refresh + sponsored local event budget request to Harry.
- **OS-driven output quality not materially better than ad-hoc:** [low likelihood given 5 days of use, but worth tracking]. Mitigation: explicit go-no-go review at end of June.

## Next month priorities

1. **Stride nail surgery campaign live by 2 June.** Paid + organic, full first-cycle measured by 30 June, success = 15 nail-surgery bookings at <£25 CPA.
2. **Master Marketing Document locked, three-way sign-off by 28 May.** Next week.
3. **Luke ambassador first three posts live across May 28 to June 4.** Sequence already filmed. Success = engagement uplift in window AND 2+ Fade Golf membership enquiries citing Luke.
4. **F&E content angle refresh by mid-June.** Target 10 posts in the month with at least one new angle tested. Success = day-pass sales return to 25+.

## Questions for Tom + Harry (decisions needed)

1. **Stride Google tag fix.** Mark has slipped twice. Options: A) hold for one more week, B) push for hard date Friday, C) manual ad-group rebuild as fallback this week. Maddie's recommendation: B with C as 24-hr fallback. Tom + Harry decision needed before 23 May.
2. **F&E direction.** Two-month soft trend. Options: A) content angle refresh and review July, B) platform/positioning rethink now. Maddie's recommendation: A, decision deferred to July if still soft.

## Appendix: data sources and method notes

- Weekly Reports: 2026-05-04, 2026-05-11, 2026-05-18, 2026-05-25
- Data Pulls: 2026-05-31 monthly pull
- Linked campaigns: Luke Ambassador Phase 1, Stride Run Club, F&E May
- **What we can / cannot measure this month:** Stride conversion accuracy pre-tag-fix carries ±20% uncertainty. F&E day-pass-to-member funnel not yet tracked. All Luke-shoot attribution is correlational not causal.
```
