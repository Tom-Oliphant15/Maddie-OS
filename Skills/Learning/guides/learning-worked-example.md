# Learning, Worked example flow

A realistic end-to-end run showing how a Learning audit moves question by question, confirms understanding before writing, and handles a park-the-rest exit.

---

```
Skill: I have read all 11 Brain files. Found 8 gaps. Top priority: Stride voice still has no reference posts, which means Campaign Brief Expander is guessing on Stride tone. Going to ask about that first. OK?

Maddie: yes

Skill:
**Stride voice, Reference Posts**

What this is: 3 to 5 Stride posts that nailed the voice. Future skills use these as anchor examples for any Stride copy they help you with.

Why it matters: Campaign Brief Expander and Cross-Platform Repurposer both check brand voice when producing output. Without reference posts, they fall back to a generic "clinical and warm" instruction which is not as sharp as the actual examples.

Current state: section is empty (placeholder text only)

My question: show me 3 to 5 Stride posts (or post drafts) that nailed the voice. Paste the text, or link to where they live on Instagram / Facebook. I will save them to the voice file.

Maddie: [pastes 4 posts]

Skill: Got them. Reading...

These 4 examples cluster around two patterns:
1. Reassurance-first ("you are not alone in this")
2. Plain-English education ("here is what is actually happening with your feet")

Just to check: are both of those patterns equally "Stride" to you, or is one more core than the other?

Maddie: reassurance is the priority, education is the support

Skill: OK, writing to Brain. Calling out reassurance-first as the core voice marker, education-supporting as secondary. Going to the next gap. Next is Fade Golf audience for the membership service, currently no persona file at all. Continue or pause?

Maddie: continue

[...skill keeps going question by question...]

Maddie: park the rest, I have 10 minutes

Skill: Parking 3 gaps for next run: [list]. 5 gaps closed today. Brain coverage moved from 64% to 81%. Saving and pushing to Tom. Done.
```
