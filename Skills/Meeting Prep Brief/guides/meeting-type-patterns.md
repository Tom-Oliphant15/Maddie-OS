# Guide: Meeting-Type-Specific Patterns

Used by [[Meeting Prep Brief]] to adjust the brief to the actual meeting on the calendar. Extracted from the skill file 2026-05-21 to keep the skill lean.

The skill detects which type from attendees and adjusts the agenda template accordingly.

---

## Weekly Tom Zoom (30 min)

- Tom-side prep: he probably has his own briefing from Tom OS. The two briefs intersect on shared priorities.
- Format: 5 min status, 10 min Maddie's blockers, 10 min strategic / cross-brand, 5 min wrap.
- Hard recurring item: Stride Google tag fix status (if still open).

## Harry 1:1 (in-person, varies)

- Harry is operationally focused, daily details, in-person rhythm.
- Format: less structured, but the prep brief still lists the 3 to 5 things Maddie wants to raise.
- Critical persona: if Harry has been blocking something multiple weeks, the brief surfaces it for Maddie to decide whether to push.

## Three-way Tom + Harry + Maddie (weekly or bi-weekly)

- This is the strategic meeting. Master Marketing Document, budget, big-bet decisions.
- Format: structured, more agenda discipline than the 1:1s.
- Lead-time prep matters more (24h before, not 2h).

## Four-way (Tom + Harry + Maddie + external, e.g. Luke)

- Brief for Maddie covers what THEY (the external) need to walk away with, on top of what Maddie wants.
- Format: lock the agenda before the meeting, share with attendees in advance.

## External partner (Mark, Jason, etc.)

- Lighter touch on internal context, heavier on the specific thing Maddie needs from them.
- Format: short brief, 3 to 4 topics max.

---

## Detection rule (explicit)

- 1 attendee + Maddie, attendee = Tom: Weekly Tom Zoom
- 1 attendee + Maddie, attendee = Harry: Harry 1:1
- 1 attendee + Maddie, attendee = any Fade Group director other than Tom or Harry: 1:1 (external context)
- 3 attendees = Tom + Harry + Maddie: Three-way strategic
- 4+ attendees including Tom + Harry + Maddie + at least one external: Four-way
- Any other configuration without Tom or Harry: External partner

If Maddie supplied attendees that don't match any pattern cleanly, ask her to confirm the type (default to External partner).
