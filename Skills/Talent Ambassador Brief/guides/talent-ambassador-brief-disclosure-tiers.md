# Guide: Talent Ambassador Brief Disclosure Tiers

The six commercial-relationship tiers used by [[Talent Ambassador Brief]] in Step 1.3 to classify the talent's relationship to the brand and pick the correct disclosure pattern. Loaded by the skill at runtime. Extracted from the skill file 2026-05-21 to keep the skill body lean.

Each tier drives both the Step 1.3 input capture and the Disclosure branch in the output template (see [[talent-ambassador-brief-template]]).

---

## The six tiers

**Paid ambassador / contracted promo.** Ambassador retainer, fee per post, any cash relationship. ASA / CAP treat this as paid partnership: full `#ad` disclosure required AND platform Paid Partnership label.

**Gifted product or service (any value).** Free product, loan, comped treatment, complimentary membership, free trip. Disclosure required regardless of value. The £100 threshold sometimes cited in industry blogs is NOT in CAP. The trigger is the commercial relationship itself, not the value. Label as `Ad - Gifted` at the start of the caption.

**Affiliate code or affiliate link.** Talent has a discount code (e.g. `LUKE10`), referral link, or any commission arrangement. Counts as a commercial relationship, disclosure required at the same placement as the link or code (in-video for video, in caption for captions, in description if link is in description).

**Employee posting brand content.** Disclosure as employee required ("I work at Fade Golf" / "as part of the team here"), visible early in the caption or overlaid in video. No `#ad` needed because the employment relationship is the disclosure, but it has to be obvious to a casual viewer.

**Friend-of-brand, no payment, no gifted product, no code, no agreement.** No statutory disclosure required, but transparency-friendly phrasing recommended ("I'm a member here", "I go to F&E"). If there is ANY agreement or expectation (even implicit), upgrade to the relevant paid / gifted tier.

**Patient (Stride only) sharing their treatment story.** Not a commercial post in the ASA sense, but signed Patient Consent + Article 9 health-data lawful basis + clinical-claim approval are all required. Routes to the patient-testimonial overlay in the output template.

---

## 12-month tail rule

If the commercial relationship has ended within the last 12 months (e.g. Luke comes off the ambassador retainer in June, posts about Fade Golf in October), disclosure still applies to that post. The lingering-association rule under ASA / CAP requires continued disclosure for up to 12 months after the formal relationship ends.

Capture this in Step 1.3 if relevant ("ambassador retainer ended [date]") and the brief inherits the paid-tier disclosure.
