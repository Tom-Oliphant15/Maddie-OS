# Guide: Talent Ambassador Brief Worked Example

Worked example used by [[Talent Ambassador Brief]]. Extracted from the skill file 2026-05-21 to keep the skill lean. Demonstrates a paid ambassador (Luke, Fade Golf) post-shoot reel sharing end-to-end, showing all three layered disclosure actions.

---

## Example: Luke ambassador reel for Fade Golf

```
Maddie: ambassador brief for Luke, the Fade Golf reel from last week

Skill: A few inputs.

Brand: Fade Golf, confirmed.
Talent: Luke, paid ambassador, golf demographic.
What he is posting: the ambassador intro reel from Wednesday's shoot. I see it referenced in Information/Filming Briefs/2026-05-21 Fade Golf Luke Ambassador.md. Pulling that for context.
Platform(s): his IG main + story?
Timing: any specific date?

Maddie: IG main and story. Post next Tuesday evening.

Skill: Building the brief.

[Skill produces a one-page brief. Because Luke is on a paid ambassador retainer, the Disclosure section instructs three layered actions: (1) `#ad` at the START of the caption (first line, first few words, visible before the "more" cut), (2) Instagram's Paid Partnership label toggle in the upload flow (the strongest defensible position, auto-discloses across feed, story, Reels), (3) for the reel itself, a text overlay reading `#ad` across the opening second to two seconds since caption-only is not adequate for short-form video. Explicitly forbidden: `#sp` / `#partner` / `#collab` / `#BrandAmbassador` / `#thanks Fade Golf`. Example caption is drafted with a note "this is a starting frame, not a script, you know your audience's voice better than I do, rewrite freely". Hashtags include #fadegolf #leamingtonspa #golftraining and Luke's own #LukeOnFade, all kept separate from the `#ad` disclosure at the start. Suggested 7pm Tuesday posting.]

Skill: Brief saved. One page, copy-pasteable. Three options:

A) Draft a WhatsApp to Luke with the brief inline
B) Trim to a 3-line version if you want to send something shorter
C) Done

Maddie: A

Skill: WhatsApp draft below. Click send.

> "Hey Luke, here is the brief for the ambassador reel post next Tuesday. Caption draft is just a starting point, rewrite in your voice if better. Let me know if anything is unclear. [pasted brief]"
```
