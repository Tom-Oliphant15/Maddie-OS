# Guide: Filming Brief Template

Full output template used by [[Filming Brief Generator]]. Extracted from the skill file 2026-05-21 to keep the skill lean. Covers the complete brief format with all 11 sections plus the follow-up tasks block.

---

## Output Format

```markdown
# Filming Brief: [Brand] - [Subject]

**Shoot date:** [date]
**Talent:** [name + role]
**Crew:** [name(s), or "self-filmed by talent" / "Maddie filming"]
**Location:** [specific]
**Primary platform:** [where the master deliverable lives]
**Secondary deliverables:** [list, with aspect ratios]
**Linked campaign:** [if any, with file link]
**Talent release status:** [signed / outstanding / blocker - filming cannot proceed without]
**Stride clinical claim sign-off (if applicable):** [signed by Harry / outstanding]
**Generated:** YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM by Filming Brief Generator

---

## 1. Overview

**Goal of this shoot:** [one sentence, drawn from campaign brief if applicable]

**Tone direction:** [from brand voice, plain English. E.g. "Reassuring and clinical-warm. Wendy on camera, not the procedure itself."]

**Authenticity note:** raw and personal beats polished. Talent speaks like they're telling a friend. Crew avoids over-staging.

**What we are trying to NOT do:** [the drift catch, drawn from voice file's Bad Examples]

**Reference:** [link or description of one named reference - existing post, competitor video, or stills that aligns the team on the look]

---

## 2. Hook and Script Architecture (talking-talent shoots)

**Hook type:** [outcome / problem-statement / pattern interrupt / curiosity]
**Exact opening line (first 3 seconds):** "[exact words]"

**Problem beat (3 to 8 sec):** [the pain or trigger in viewer's words, drawn from persona Triggers]

**Solution beats (8 to 30 sec):** [3 to 5 talking points]
1. [...]
2. [...]
3. [...]

**CTA (last 5 sec):** "[exact words, value exchange]"

**Retention pacing target:** cuts every 2 to 4 seconds. Alternate frames. Never same shot longer than 3 sentences. Target watch-through 70%+.

---

## 3. Shotlist (9:16 vertical first)

Numbered list. Priority order (essential first, nice-to-have last).

**#1 | [Shot name]**
- Frame: [wide / medium / close]
- Angle: [eye-level / low / overhead / behind]
- Aspect: 9:16 master [or flag if 16:9-only deliverable]
- Length: [approx seconds]
- Description: [what is happening, what talent is doing, what is in frame]
- Purpose: [what this shot is for in the final cut]
- Hook beat it serves: [Hook / Problem / Solution / CTA / B-roll]

[continue for 8 to 15 shots]

---

## 4. Sequence (filming order)

Order to capture for production efficiency:

1. Setup [location/setup 1]
2. Capture shots [#X, #Y, #Z] (same setup)
3. Move to [next setup]
4. Capture shots [#A, #B]
...

This is the order to FILM, not the order shots will appear in the final cut.

---

## 5. Talking Points (for talent)

For each speaking beat, give talent:
- **Topic:** [what they are talking about]
- **Key phrases to include:** [3 to 5 specific phrases]
- **Phrases to avoid:** [brand voice avoid list + campaign-specific no-go zones]
- **Tone direction:** [conversational, reassuring, energetic, per brand voice]

**Talking points are NEVER a script.** Talent speaks naturally; points anchor what to cover.

---

## 6. B-roll (grouped by purpose)

**Detail shots** (close-ups, product / craft / hands):
- [item] - feeds: [future content this might support]
- [item] - feeds: [...]

**Environment shots** (location, setting, atmosphere):
- [item] - feeds: [...]
- [item] - feeds: [...]

**Ambient activity** (people doing things in the space, not directed):
- [item] - feeds: [...]

**Transition shots** (movement, doorways, hands picking up objects - useful for edit transitions):
- [item] - feeds: [...]

5 to 12 B-roll items typically. These survive beyond one campaign.

---

## 7. Sound

- **Dialogue:** [lav mic on talent preferred, on-camera mic acceptable, phone mic last resort with note]
- **Ambient:** [specific ambient to capture, e.g. "30 sec empty bay sound, 30 sec crowded bay sound"]
- **Music direction:** [if specified, otherwise "to be added in post - upbeat-but-not-cliched, royalty-free pool"]
- **Watch for:** [specific sound risks, e.g. "echo in F&E hall", "wind on outdoor shoots", "clinic background noise"]

---

## 8. Captions and Post-Production

- **Burned-in captions:** YES (assumed for all short-form). Style: [bold sans-serif, white with shadow, centre-vertical safe zone, 1 to 2 lines max, current word highlighted in brand colour].
- **Caption timing:** every word appears as spoken, no lag.
- **Cut style:** [fast 2 to 4 sec / measured 4 to 6 sec / single-take feel for select content]
- **Pacing:** [seconds per shot guidance, retention target 70%+ watch-through]
- **Text overlays beyond captions:** [headlines, callouts - if yes, specify style]
- **End frame:** [logo lock-up 1 sec / CTA card / none - default: no logo end-card on short-form, kills replay rate]
- **Aspect ratio master:** 9:16 (1080x1920)
- **Secondary crops needed:** [1:1 / 16:9 listed if applicable]

---

## 9. UK Consent and Usage Rights (documents generated)

**Releases generated for this shoot:**

| Person | Release type | File path | Status | Deadline |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| [Talent name] | Talent Release | Information/Talent Releases/Drafts/[YYYY-MM-DD] [Brand] [Name] - Talent Release.docx | Drafted, awaiting signature | T-3 |
| [Patient name, if Stride] | Stride Patient Consent | Information/Talent Releases/Drafts/[YYYY-MM-DD] Stride [Name] - Patient Consent.docx | Drafted, awaiting signature | T-3 |

**Pre-shoot blocker:** filming cannot proceed for any person whose signed release is not back by T-3.

**Usage scope agreed per person:** see the populated .docx for full detail. Summary:
- **Platforms in scope:** [list, e.g. organic social, paid social, brand website]
- **Territory:** [UK / Europe / Worldwide]
- **Duration:** [12 months / 24 months / perpetual / etc.]
- **Talent own posting:** [yes / no]
- **Compensation:** [as agreed]

**Background-incidental people:**
- [F&E customers in frame: venue signage confirmed / film during closed setup / blur in post]
- [Fade Golf members in frame: same approach]
- [Run Club public in frame: shoot in controlled angle / verbal consent on the day]

**Stride patient-specific:**
- Patient Consent template used (NOT general Talent Release)
- Clinical claim Harry sign-off: [confirmed / outstanding - BLOCKER]
- Before/after consent: separate explicit box ticked on form
- Identifiability level chosen per patient

**Data handling:**
- Raw footage stored on [Maddie's drive, access-controlled]
- Withdrawal route per release form: 30-day removal from owned channels
- GDPR lawful basis: consent (Article 6(1)(a) for talent; Article 9(2)(a) for Stride patient health-context content)

---

| Deliverable | Platform | Aspect | Length | Caption style | CTA | Target post date |

## Follow-up tasks (auto-added to Tasks.md)

- [Date-7] Confirm talent release signed; if not, push or replace talent
- [Date-3] Call sheet distributed to talent + crew
- [Date-1] Equipment check, batteries charged, storage cleared
- [Date+1] Review footage, organise into folders by shotlist number
- [Date+3] First-cut edit of [primary deliverable]
- [Date+5] Tone-check captions for each post, ensure no em / en dashes
- [Date+7] Schedule across platforms
```
