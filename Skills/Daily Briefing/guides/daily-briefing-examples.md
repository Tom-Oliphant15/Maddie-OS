# Daily Briefing, Worked Examples

Two reference briefings for tone and depth. Quiet day to show the critical persona pushing for honesty about what is actually live. Busy day to show the meeting-driven, deadline-driven version.

## Quiet day example

```markdown
# Daily Log, 2026-05-26

## Morning Briefing (07:42)

### 1. Diary
**Today (Mon 26 May):** No meetings.
**Tomorrow (Tue 27 May):** 16:00, Master Marketing Document deep work session with Tom.

### 2. Messages for today
Nothing in your inboxes needs a reply today.
Slack: zero @mentions, two new posts in #fade-group-marketing (Tom dropping Apify research), no DMs.

### 3. Top 3 today
1. **Stride Google tag verification with Mark.** Hard blocker on the per-service Ads restructure. He said yesterday he was about to push the fix.
2. **Master Marketing Document Stride section draft.** Tomorrow's deep work needs a draft in hand for the per-service positioning conversation.
3. **Reply to Britt's influencer template DM.** Sat at 3 days, she will assume it is parked.

### 4. Weekly checklist
| Brand | Posts this week | Active campaign | Tools |
|---|---|---|---|
| Fade Golf | 1 (target 3) | Luke ambassador, behind | check |
| Stride | 2 (target 5) | Run Club, on track | WordPress access still blocked |
| Fire & Earth | 0 (target 2) | None | check |

Two things to flag: Fire & Earth at zero posts in a 7-day window is the lowest I have seen since you started. Worth deciding whether to push something or accept the gap. And Fade Golf needs its Luke campaign content moving, second week behind.

### 5. To-do (next 10)
... [10 items]

### 6. Awaiting others
**Tom:** Britt influencer template architecture (not blocking yet)
**Harry:** WordPress admin login (12 days overdue, chase today?)
**Mark:** Stride Google tag fix (today per yesterday)

### 7. Recommendations
1. **Chase Harry on WordPress today.** 12 days is too long. One Slack DM closes it. I can draft.
2. **Drop F&E from this week's posting target.** Better to skip a week than push something thin. Lock the decision so it does not lurk.
```
