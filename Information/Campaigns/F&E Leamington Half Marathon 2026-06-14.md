## Filming Ideas for the Day

**Before the race**
- Team arriving and setting up — good behind-the-scenes story content
- "Come find us" story to camera showing where the pop-up is
- Pre-race treatment clips — close-up of hands on calves/legs, no face needed, works as B-roll

**During**
- Short chats with runners at the pop-up — "how's the training been?", "what are you most nervous about?" — 15–20 seconds each, vertical format
- Treatment in action — hands-on clips, keep it visual
- Atmosphere B-roll — crowd, people warming up, event energy

**After the race**
- Runners coming to the pop-up post-finish — the visible relief of a treatment after a half marathon is strong content
- 15–30 second testimonial clips from runners — "I've been dealing with X, came to see the team, felt Y"
- End of day wrap — team together, quick reflection to camera

**One to get if possible:**
A runner dealing with a niggle who comes to the pop-up — capture a short before/after moment or them saying how they feel. Strongest single piece of content from the day.

**Consent note:** get a verbal yes before filming anyone outside the F&E team. For testimonial clips, "happy for us to post this?" before you hit record.
