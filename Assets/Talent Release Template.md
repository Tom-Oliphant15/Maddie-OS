# Talent Release Template

*Source-of-truth template for a simple, friendly, UK GDPR-compliant permission form. Designed to fit on one printed A4 page. The Filming Brief Generator populates a copy per person per shoot and produces a Word document. Edit here only when the form itself needs to change.*

*Variables use double-brace syntax: `{{VARIABLE}}`. The skill substitutes before writing the .docx.*

---

# Permission to use your photo and video

**{{BRAND_NAME}}**

Thanks for being part of {{SHOOT_DESCRIPTION}} on {{SHOOT_DATE}} at {{SHOOT_LOCATION}}. We'd like your permission to use the photos and video we record on the day. Please read below, ask any questions, then sign at the bottom. We'll give you a copy.

---

**Your details**

Name: {{TALENT_NAME}}

Email: {{TALENT_EMAIL}}

Phone: {{TALENT_PHONE}}

---

**What we'd like to do with the content**

Use photos and video featuring you in our marketing, specifically:

{{USAGE_LIST}}

**For how long:** {{DURATION}}

**Where:** {{TERRITORY}}

{{TALENT_OWN_POSTING_LINE}}

We will NOT use your image to train AI models or create a digital replica / deepfake of you.

---

**Your right to change your mind**

You can withdraw your permission at any time by emailing {{WITHDRAWAL_CONTACT_EMAIL}}. We'll remove the content from our own channels within 30 days. We can't always get it back from places others have shared it, but we'll stop using it ourselves straight away.

---

**By signing, you confirm:**

- You're 18 or over (or a parent / guardian has signed too)
- You're happy for us to use the content as described above
- The information above is accurate
